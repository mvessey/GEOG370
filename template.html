Homework 7: Census data choropleth

Madeline Vessey

This map displays the number of individuals with a bachelor's degree or higher in the age group 25 to 34, grouped by County in Maryland. This data was collected by the United States Census and depicts the 2015-2019 American Community Survey's 5-Year Estimates. This map is in the 1983 State Plane Maryland projection in feet, ESRI: 102685.
