Homework 8: Geoprocessing Analysis

Madeline Vessey

This map explores the question of where in North Carolina are coyote territories, based on reported coyote sightings and the typical distance of coyote territory. Do to this I had to use intersect() to isolate the dataset on coyote sightings to only sightings in North Carolina with a NC counties shapefile. Then in order to determine where territories may be, I had to add a 2.2 mile buffer around each point.
